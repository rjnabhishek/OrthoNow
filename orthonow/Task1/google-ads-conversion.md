# Google Ads Conversion Action

## Selected Conversion Event

**consultation_form_submitted**

## Why this conversion?

I would import the **consultation_form_submitted** event into Google Ads because it represents a completed lead submission by a potential patient. This is the primary business goal of the landing page and directly measures successful lead generation.

Unlike events such as **book_appointment_click**, **call_now_click**, or **whatsapp_chat_click**, which only indicate user interest, **consultation_form_submitted** confirms that the user has provided their details and requested a consultation. This makes it a high-quality conversion event for campaign optimization.

By importing this conversion into Google Ads, Smart Bidding strategies such as **Maximize Conversions** or **Target CPA** can optimize campaigns toward users who are more likely to complete the consultation form rather than simply clicking buttons or visiting pages.

## Why not the other events?

- **book_appointment_click** – Indicates interest but not a completed lead.
- **call_now_click** – User may click without actually making or completing a call.
- **whatsapp_chat_click** – Opens WhatsApp but does not guarantee an enquiry.
- **clinic_page_view** – Measures engagement, not conversion.
- **blog_scroll_90** – Useful for content analysis but not for lead generation.
- **patient_guide_submit** – Generates leads but is a secondary conversion compared to a consultation request.

Therefore, **consultation_form_submitted** is the most appropriate conversion action for Google Ads optimization because it directly aligns with the business objective of increasing qualified consultation enquiries.
